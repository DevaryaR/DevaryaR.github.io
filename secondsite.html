Animal Profiles

Elephant

Habitat: Grasslands and forests

Diet: Herbivore

Conservation Status: Endangered

Elephants are the largest land animals on Earth. They are known for their intelligence, social behavior, and strong family bonds.

Tiger

Habitat: Forests and grasslands

Diet: Carnivore

Conservation Status: Endangered

Tigers are the largest cat species in the world. They are known for their power, strength, and distinctive orange and black stripes.

Giraffe

Habitat: Savannas and woodlands

Diet: Herbivore

Conservation Status: Vulnerable

Giraffes are the tallest mammals on Earth. Their long necks allow them to reach leaves high up in trees that other herbivores cannot access.
